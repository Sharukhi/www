### (Public archive)

Lofi-Box is an extension that brings lo-fi music right into your web browser. Lofi-Box allows you to quickly access and enjoy lo-fi music while browsing the internet.

### Features

- Privacy focused
- Lightweight and fast
- Minimal UI
